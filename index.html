HOW MANY PLAYERS?

How well do you know the ELT and the BOD?

Prepare to find out! One player at a time, you first be shown a photo of a potential ELT or BOD Member. Take a good look at them! By clicking on the photograph, 5 buttons will be displayed - 4 potential names, and a button that lets you guess that the pictured person is NOT an ELT or BOD Member! There are fake pictures and fake names included to make this harder, so pay close attention! Correct answers will be logged at the bottom of the screen as the game progresses. The player or players with the most correct answers at the end of the game, wins! Good luck!
